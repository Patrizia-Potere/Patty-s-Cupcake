Ingredients

For the cupcake

  • 250g unsalted butter, softened
  • 250g caster sugar
  • 250g self-raising flour
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 4 tablespoons milk
  • Pinch of salt (optional)

For the buttercream

  • 200 g butter block softened,
  • 400 g Icing sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1-2 tbsp Milk
